Health Insurance Risk Calculator

Before we begin, here's some instructions:

This calculator is meant to simulate the calculators used by health insurance providers to determine your health risk category. Your height and weight are needed to calculate your Body Mass Index, typically referred to as your BMI. Your age, BMI, blood pressure, and history of family disease are all relevant factors in determining this placement. Following each statement or question, you will be asked to either input or select some generic medical information about yourself. Upon filling out the entire form, clicking the submit button will analyze your answers and calculate which of the four risk categories most accurately describes you (low, moderate, high, and uninsurable). Please note that this a very generic form and is not sponsored (currently) by any insurance company, if you are seeking medical coverage, do not use this as precise basis.
